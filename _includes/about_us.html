About Us

Our goal at NU Public Speaking Club (NUPS) is to empower members to become more effective communicators and leaders. We do this by providing a supportive and positive environment where each member can grow and become more confident in public speaking while developing leadership skills. At our weekly meetings, members take on various roles to give prepared and/or impromptu speeches, provide feedback to other speakers, and facilitate these meetings. We look forward to seeing you at our next meeting!
